Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Summary: TradeCalc Pro does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

TradeCalc Pro is a standalone calculator application. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Financial or trade data you enter into the calculator
  • Any user-generated content

2. Local Data Storage

All calculations and data entered into TradeCalc Pro are processed and stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to the developer
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

3. Third-Party Services

TradeCalc Pro does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
